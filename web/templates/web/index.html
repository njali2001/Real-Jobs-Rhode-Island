Program Report

Real Jobs RI grows business-led partnerships that build workforce solutions to address their unique workforce challenges. Solutions can include:

  • Placing new employees into immediate job openings;
  • Upskilling current employees to advance skills and/or remain competitive;
  • Creating pipelines of talent for the future.

Real Jobs RI is a win-win because companies get the talent they need to compete globally and grow locally, and Rhode Islanders get opportunities to thrive in a flourishing economy.
